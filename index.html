Beginner's Guide

Start by growing crops like parsnips and potatoes. Build friendships by gifting villagers items they love. Explore the caves to gather resources early!

Advanced Tips

Plan your farm with automated irrigation using sprinklers. Maximize profits by planting high-yield crops for each season!

Fishing Secrets

Master the fishing mini-game! Use bait and tackle for better results. Watch for different fish available during seasons and weather conditions.

Festivals Guide

Attend festivals to win prizes and unlock rare items. During the Egg Festival, buy strawberries to plant and profit all spring!
